## Company Overview

Duolingo is a consumer learning product with a freemium model and subscription offering. Its growth system is useful for studying how product habit, brand, international reach, monetization, and experimentation can reinforce one another.

The current coverage combines Gina Gotthilf's account of organic growth, brand, subscription levers, and internationalization with Albert Cheng's cross-company frameworks for explore-exploit decisions, retention, experimentation, and gamification.

## Evidence and Limits

- Current evidence relies primarily on participant interviews rather than complete internal experiment and cohort data.
- Duolingo's public growth cannot be attributed to brand, gamification, or any single person or mechanism.
- Methods reflect a high-frequency consumer learning product and may not transfer to low-frequency or enterprise products.
- Inclusion does not endorse every company decision, campaign, or product claim.

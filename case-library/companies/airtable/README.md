## Company Overview

Airtable is a collaborative platform that can support many workflows. That flexibility creates a growth challenge: a new user must understand what to build, reach value without excessive setup, and often bring other people into the workflow.

The current coverage connects two participant perspectives. Zoelle Egner discusses early customer-led marketing, brand, templates, and feedback. Lauryn Isford explains onboarding, activation, experimentation, and downstream collaboration measures.

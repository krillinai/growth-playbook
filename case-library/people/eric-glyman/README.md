## Core Growth Ideas

### 1. Anchor Growth in Customer Savings

Ramp's stated proposition is to help companies spend less and operate finance more efficiently. This differs from card models that benefit when customers spend more or carry balances.

### 2. Align the Business Model With the Promise

A differentiated message is credible only when pricing, product behavior, and revenue incentives support it. Teams should test whether customers realize measurable savings rather than rely on positioning alone.

### 3. Use Speed and Focus Together

Urgency compounds when the team concentrates on a clear customer problem. Speed without focus spreads resources and increases operating risk.

### 4. Apply AI to Operational Work

AI can categorize, review, route, and automate finance workflows. Value should be measured through accuracy, time saved, control quality, customer trust, and total cost.

## Attribution Rules

- Prefer full talks, the coauthored book, and direct teaching material over clips and vendor summaries.
- Attribute the book to Ronny Kohavi, Diane Tang, and Ya Xu.
- Separate Kohavi's methods from interviewer framing, co-speaker claims, and company outcomes.
- Do not copy long passages. Summarize the method and link the source.
- Do not imply endorsement, affiliation, private access, or current company facts.
- High-risk statistical, legal, ethical, medical, financial, employment, or safety decisions require appropriate expert review.
